Medical Profile Assistant

Provide best quality healthcare for you

It’s a useful software helping to improve the medical system in Egypt, by decreasing the time taken in examine the patient. First every person should create his profile on the software and adding basic information about himself, this information including his personal information like: name, age, gender, job, relative’s mobile number, doctor’s name or mobile number or address. And his medical information like: blood type, any medications he use to take, any diseases he have. And his medical history like: any surgeries he had done before. When you visit a doctor you won’t have to answer many questions, all you have to do is to show him your profile and he will get all the information he need. This will save time and make doctors help Injured people very quickly and save more lives. Also we can connect this software with a QR code in the shape wooden or plastic Medal so if any one loses his conscious or injured at an accident all you need is to scan his QR code medal and you will reach his profile, that will save time and help you to take the right decision and help him quickly, and reach to his relatives, and will help ambulance men to icrease the chance of saving his life
